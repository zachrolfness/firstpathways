Creating Pathways through all levels of the FIRST® Robotics Competition

Welcome to FIRST Pathways!

FIRST Pathways , a program started by the FIRST Dean’s List class of 2017, aims to encourage the development of FIRST by providing an outlet for teams to collaborate and grow the FIRST program both locally and globally. Pathways intends to create a model system that all teams can adopt for their own team to implement within their school districts. By working together, we aim to connect all levels of FIRST Robotics and promote STEM as a life long program.

Why Pathways

FIRST® Pathways seeks to create a unified methodology and program providing FRC teams the necessary tools to help establish FLL Jr., FLL, FTC and finally FRC feeder programs in their school district and their communities. By providing resources, organizational support, and funding model, Pathways aims to fully realize Dean Kamen’s goal for the “Sport of the Mind”.


There are many benefits to this system. Firstly, it would provide students with access to all levels of FIRST® throughout their academic career. Secondly, it establishes a sustainability system for all levels of the team making sure that there is a constant pipeline of students. Thirdly, it directly puts FIRST® Robotics into the school community and changes the culture to become a part of the student identity. Fourthly, it better prepares students for high school robotics as well as gives them experience with the lower levels making them more inclined to mentor teams once they are at the higher level. Finally, it embodies Dean Kamen’s goal of creating the Sport of the Mind by giving students a lifelong sport that they can grow up, make lifelong friends in, and ultimately learn and be inspired by science and technology.


Progression of Programs

The FIRST Pathways Progression of Programs encourages Pathways teams to create a relationship with their respective school districts to develop the levels of FIRST throughout the community. Pathways teams would mentor the younger level teams and in doing so, bridge the gap between levels, lessening the drop off rate. This system would create a cyclical system of students growing through FIRST, and as students graduate they pass down the inspiration and knowledge to the younger students involved in the pathway.

Resource Database

As FIRST Pathways aims to create a consolidation of resources for all levels of FIRST, Pathways teams can upload resources to be shared worldwide. Whether videos, activities, or guides, these resources will undergo a checking process before publishing as to ensure teams accessing the resources will be given the most accurate information possible. This also allows teams to become fully involved with FIRST Pathways while in the beginning stages of building a progression system.
